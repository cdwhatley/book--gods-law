Appendix A: Rapid Decline from Biblical Morality in the US

This appendix contains quotes from the website Revival Outside the Walls Ministry.

The following is a quote from the ROTW website concerning their collection of statistics.

ROTW Certified Statistics represent the evidence-based consensus of three or more respected research studies, compiled over five years and thousands of hours of research on all church-related studies, by Dr. Doug Peterson, Oxford Center for Mission Studies.

These statistics reveal the shocking status of Christianity in America today, and the biblical illiteracy of most of those who call themselves Christians. These statistics confirm the urgent need for Igniting Christians to Ignite America with Revival, by moving everyone, every day, closer to Jesus.

Doug Peterson is the Margaret S. Smith Distinguished Professor of World Mission and Intercultural Studies and a director of the Judkins Institute for Leadership at Vanguard University. With more than 30 years of leadership responsibilities in non-profit and higher education organization in Latin America and the US.


The following are some of the statistics posted on the ROTW website.

  • 65% of Christians believe there are multiple paths to Heaven.
  • 51% of Evangelicals believe many religions can lead to eternal life.
  • Almost 50% of all Evangelicals, 18-29 years of age, favor same sex marriage.
  • Two-thirds of those that are 18-34 years of age, do not believe pre-marital sex is ever wrong.
  • More than 50% of those 18-24 years of age are not certain they believe in God.
  • The Nones (religiously unaffiliated) are now the 2nd largest religious group in the US. After Evangelicals, they lead Oregon by 37% and New Hampshire by 35%.
  • 44% of US adults say that God plays no role whatsoever in their life.
  • 69% of church goers believe that everyone will go to Heaven.
